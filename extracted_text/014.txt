De weduwe Van Zanten-Kappers heeft nog drie kinderen thuis wonen: Jantje, Antje en Bessel,
In de kolom "kerkelijke gezinte” is aangetekend: "tot geen der genoemde gezindheden behorend”. Welke dan wel, blijft

Summary
Aart van Zanten is not required to serve in the army. He works as mason.
Previous to her marriage Anna Antonia Kappers works as maid. In the population records for the widow A.A. Kappers is
under religion written ‘none of the above’, which means neither Roman Catholic, nor Dutch Reformed. But which
denomination, if any, remains unclear.
